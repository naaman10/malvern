Hugo Kimber

Executive Chairman

As Executive Chairman of Malvern, I am responsible to the wider Group and our shareholders for the success and performance of the business and our brands.

Life before Malvern…

I started work in the travel industry after university, where I did a degree in Indian History. This first role was with Cox and Kings and I worked for them in the UK, Russia and India for nearly 10 years.

I have since grown and managed companies in the travel industry and set up a consultancy to support the industry on sustainability.

I returned to Cox & Kings over 4 years ago, under which I subsequently established the Malvern Group.

Across my career I have enjoyed creating new products, including a luxury steam train in Russia, award-winning carbon management software and walking trips in the Indian Himalayas, as well as transforming a number of companies. Above all, I enjoy working with people to make things happen.

What’s your favourite UK destination?

Branscombe on the Jurassic coast - great pub Mason's Arms, beach and walks.

Jon Ross

Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

I’m the Chief Technology Officer of the Malvern Group. I look after IT development, service and support as well as the Digital Transformation team who are responsible for innovation on our platforms.

Life before Malvern…

I’ve spent much of my career to date as an e-commerce focused consultant working with companies such as Barclays Bank, Moneysupermarket and GE, as well as spending long periods in America, Europe and the Middle-East.

I moved from consultancy to work for Phones4U where I was responsible for building their online channel before initially joining LateRooms.com in 2014 and later Malvern in August 2016, after hearing the impressive vision for the future of the group.

What’s your favourite UK destination?

Saddleworth – It’s on my doorstep but on a sunny day with the right view you could believe that you are in the hills of Tuscany and still be home for bedtime.

John McLaughlin

Chief Finance Officer (CFO)

As CFO for Malvern, I’m responsible for both the finance and commercial functions across the Group and our brands.

Life before Malvern…

Before joining Malvern I worked at TalkTalk where I was the Commercial Finance Director. Prior to TalkTalk I worked in various technology and telecommunications companies across the North West, along with a stint in Investment Banking.

What’s your favourite UK destination?

The Lake District – I’ve been taking my children there since they were born.
